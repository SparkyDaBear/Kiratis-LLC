# Kiratis LLC Page Outline

## Home

Goal: give a fast, elegant overview and route visitors to the right service.

Sections:

- Simple hero with a concise business statement.
- Three prominent links or cards for Consulting, Junk Removal, and Custom Computer Builds.
- Short trust block with experience, process, or outcomes.
- Closing call to action for contact or quote request.

Hero copy direction:

- Lead with the business name and the value proposition.
- Keep the hero short and uncluttered.
- Use one primary action and one secondary action.

## Consulting

Goal: present the technical consulting work as the premium offering.

Sections:

- Intro that explains biophysical modeling and metadata harmonization in plain language.
- Service summary covering protein folding simulations, statistical modeling, encoder-decoder pipelines, and tool development.
- Deliverables or engagement examples.
- Why this service matters section focused on value, accuracy, and workflow quality.
- Contact prompt for consulting inquiries.

Suggested tone:

- Credible.
- Technical, but understandable.
- Sharp and concise.

## Junk Removal

Goal: provide a straightforward local service page modeled after a typical independent junk removal company in State College, PA.

Sections:

- Simple service intro.
- Common job types such as cleanouts, haul-away, and single-item pickup.
- Local service area block.
- Fast quote or call-to-action section.
- Optional FAQ or service notes if needed later.

Suggested tone:

- Practical.
- Local.
- Direct and easy to scan.

## Custom Computer Builds

Goal: showcase available builds and make inventory updates easy to manage.

Sections:

- Intro explaining the build offerings.
- Build list or cards driven from CSV.
- Clear component summaries.
- Availability and pricing.
- Optional comparison or filtering later if the inventory grows.

Suggested tone:

- Clear.
- Product-focused.
- Slightly enthusiast-friendly without becoming jargon heavy.

## Contact

Goal: make it easy to reach the business and route inquiries correctly.

Sections:

- Contact details loaded from JSON.
- Service-specific inquiry options.
- Business email and phone placeholders.
- Optional form if needed later.

## Visual Notes

- The home hero should stay minimal and direct.
- Use modern science styling with artistic visual accents.
- Keep the pages consistent in typography, spacing, and CTA treatment.
- Let service-specific content vary, but keep the overall system unified.
